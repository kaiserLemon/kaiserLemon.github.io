About Me

I am a PhD student at CEA and Polytechnic Institute of Paris, focused on autonomous task planning under constraints for robotics applications. My research sits at the intersection of symbolic AI and robotics.

Previously, I completed a Master's degree in Computer Science at Sorbonne University and a Bachelor's degree at Nantes University, where I built a strong foundation in machine learning, optimization, and software engineering.

I have hands-on industry experience through internships at Safran Electronics & Defense and research positions at ISIR (Sorbonne) and LS2N (Nantes).
